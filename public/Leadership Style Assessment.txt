LEADERSHIP STYLE INTERPRETATION
1. Autocratic Leader
Decisive, control-oriented, efficient in crises. May limit creativity and engagement.
2. Democratic Leader
Inclusive, collaborative, trust-building. Slower decision-making in urgent situations.
3. Laissez-Faire Leader
Empowers autonomy, suits expert teams. Risk of lack of direction.
4. Transformational Leader
Vision-driven, inspirational, change-oriented. Requires emotional intelligence and consistency.
5. Transactional Leader
Performance-focused, structured, result-oriented. Limited emotional connection.
6. Servant Leader
People-first, ethical, trust-based. May struggle with authority enforcement.
7. Situational Leader
Flexible, adaptive, context-driven. Requires high awareness.
8. Coaching Leader
Development-focused, patient, growth-oriented. Time-intensive.
9. Visionary Leader
Future-focused, innovative, purpose-driven. Needs strong execution support.
10. Bureaucratic Leader
Process-driven, reliable, compliance-focused. Low flexibility.
