1) GLOBAL ENGLISH PROFICIENCY MASTER PROGRAM
====================================================================

IELTS | TOEFL | PTE | CELPIP

20 Hours | 4 Modules | 5 Hours Each


-- TARGET LEARNERS --

Students & professionals aiming for international study,
migration, work, or permanent residency.


-- COURSE PROMISE --

To build real English proficiency and test confidence,
not just exam tricks.


--------------------------------------------------------------------
COURSE STRUCTURE OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------

LISTENING   : 5 Hours | Accuracy, prediction, focus
READING     : 5 Hours | Speed, comprehension, strategy
WRITING     : 5 Hours | Structure, coherence, band improvement
SPEAKING    : 5 Hours | Fluency, confidence, readiness


-- INCLUDED ACROSS ALL MODULES --

- Study material provided
- Regular practice tasks
- Mock tests
- Writing assessments & feedback
- Speaking evaluations


--------------------
MODULE 1: LISTENING MASTERY (5 HOURS)
--------------------

Topics Covered:
- Listening test formats (IELTS / TOEFL / PTE / CELPIP)
- MCQs
- Sentence completion
- Form / note completion
- Matching
- Maps & diagrams
- Identifying distractors
- Handling fast speech & unfamiliar vocabulary
- Note-taking strategies
- Time management


--------------------
MODULE 2: READING MASTERY (5 HOURS)
--------------------

Topics Covered:
- Reading formats (all exams)
- Skimming & scanning
- True / False / Not Given
- Yes / No / Not Given
- Matching headings
- Sentence & summary completion
- MCQs
- Vocabulary in context
- Paraphrasing recognition
- Time allocation per passage


--------------------
MODULE 3: WRITING MASTERY (5 HOURS)
--------------------

Topics Covered:

TASK 1:
- Academic: graphs, charts, processes, maps
- General: letters (formal / semi-formal / informal)
- Report structure
- Data comparison & trend language

TASK 2:
- Essay types: opinion, discussion, problem–solution,
  advantage–disadvantage
- Planning before writing
- Paragraph structure
- Introductions & conclusions
- Cohesive devices
- Avoiding common grammar errors


--------------------
MODULE 4: SPEAKING MASTERY (5 HOURS)
--------------------

Topics Covered:
- Speaking formats (all exams)
- Introductions & warm-up
- Cue card / long response
- Discussion & opinion questions
- Fluency-building techniques
- Pronunciation & intonation
- Expanding answers naturally
- Handling difficult questions
- Thinking-time strategies
